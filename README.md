Dentist Next Door — 

A web platform connecting verified specialist dental consultants with clinic owners across India.

About
Dentist Next Door is a health-tech marketplace that makes it simple for dental clinics to find and book specialist consultants on demand — and for consultant dentists to showcase their expertise and grow their practice.
